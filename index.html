About

The Pitch Perfect: Corparenza Innovation Challenge is North Bengal’s premier platform for aspiring entrepreneurs, hosted by the School of Management at Inspiria Knowledge Campus. This flagship event aims to provide a dynamic space where students and budding entrepreneurs can pitch their innovative business ideas to a panel of industry experts. It stands out as one of the largest management fest events in the region, designed to inspire creativity and foster the spirit of entrepreneurship..

Key Oppurtunities for Participants

Mentorship


Guidance from experienced professionals to help refine business ideas.

Networking


Connections with potential investors and fellow entrepreneurs for future collaborations.

Incubation


Support to help develop and launch business ideas successfully.

Prizes


Awards including the Champion Trophy and free registration for Techstars Startup Weekend Siliguri 2025.

Structure

  • 20th November 2024 – 28th February 2025
    Registration Period
    Registration for the Pitch Perfect: Corparenza Innovation Challenge opens on November 20, 2024. Aspiring entrepreneurs, students, mentors, and industry professionals can register until February 28, 2025.
  • 15th February 2025
    Pre-Event Information Session
    A virtual session will be held to guide participants on the pitching process, Lean Canvas framework, and other event details. This session will also include helpful tips and resources for preparing a strong pitch.
  • 28th February 2025
    Final Registration Deadline
    The registration period for the Pitch Perfect: Corparenza Innovation Challenge will officially close.
  • 1st March 2025
    Distribution of Pitch Deck Template
    All registered participants will receive a pitch deck template to help them structure and refine their presentations.
  • 6th March 2025
    Event Day Instructions
    Participants will receive final instructions regarding their pitch slot timings, venue, and virtual meeting details for the event day.
  • 9th March 2025
    Event Day
    Participants present their business ideas to judges, compete for the Champion Trophy, and connect with potential mentors and investors. The event includes networking sessions, an award ceremony, and mentorship announcements.
  • 11th March 2025 – 1st April 2025
    Post-Event Mentorship and Incubation Support
    Winners begin their journey with tailored mentorship sessions, networking events, and access to incubation resources to develop their startup ideas further.
